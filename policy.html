INFORMATION WE COLLECT.

When you use or create an account using our App, we only collect personal information that you provide to us. Please be advised that public personal data can show up when someone does a search on another search engine. In some cases, people you share and communicate with may download or copy the content you provided to them or make it public. Use caution about the personal information you share with others.

Personal Information. When you create an account, you will provide personal information that identifies you, whether directly or indirectly, such as such as your name, address, phone number, date of birth, gender, email and interests, job, education, your profile information under “about me”, payment information, and city and country of origin. Your Content. The personal information (such as username, user ID, profile picture, and photos) you provide via your account will generate content (“Your Content”). Personal Information Required by Law. We also collect personal information that we are required or authorized by applicable laws to collect and process in order to authenticate or identify you or to verify the personal information we have collected.

Your Payment Information. When you make a payment, you may need to provide financial account information, such as your credit card number. Your Automatic Data Collection. Internet, Network, and Telecommunication. Communication. We will provide you reasonable mechanisms to exercise your choice regarding receipt of information from Imcovery.
